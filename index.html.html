Fama Barber Shop and Beauty Salon

Style meets elegance. Confidence begins here.

Welcome to Fama Barber Shop, where tradition meets trend. Our skilled barbers and beauticians offer premium grooming services in a relaxing, professional atmosphere. Whether it’s a classic haircut, a fresh beard trim, or a rejuvenating facial, we’ve got you covered.

Our Services

💈 Haircut

Experience the precision of our professional stylists who understand your unique style. Whether it's a classic cut or a modern fade, we deliver clean, tailored results that turn heads.

🧔 Beard Trim

Shape, define, and sharpen your beard with our expert grooming. We blend style with care using top-tier tools to give you a crisp, clean finish.

💆 Facial

Revitalize your skin with our rejuvenating facial services. Designed to cleanse, exfoliate, and hydrate, our treatments leave your face glowing and refreshed.

🚿 Hair Wash

Enjoy a relaxing wash that cleanses your scalp and restores your hair's natural shine. We use premium shampoos and conditioners suited for all hair types.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your working hours?

We’re open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Sundays we're closed.

Do I need an appointment or can I walk in?

We accept both walk-ins and appointments. For quicker service, we recommend booking in advance.

Are your products suitable for sensitive skin?

Absolutely! We use high-quality, hypoallergenic products suited for all skin types.

Do you offer grooming services for kids?

Yes, we offer stylish and gentle haircuts for kids of all ages.
